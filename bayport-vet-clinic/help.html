Using Bayport

Your sidebar is tailored to your role. Hover any menu item for a short description. Primary actions on the dashboard use a blue highlight.

Common tasks

  • Appointments — Book visits, approve requests, and mark visits done.
  • Consultations — Follow the numbered steps: diagnosis, procedures, and save the visit.
  • Pet profiles — Open highlights at the top; expand sections for owner details and full medical history.
  • Billing & POS — Use the fee catalog where available for faster checkout.

Troubleshooting

  • If you see a red banner about the backend, confirm the clinic server is running and your network connection is stable.
  • If login fails after a password change, ask an administrator to verify your account is active.
  • For receipt printers on desktop, pick a device in Settings after installing the Bayport print helper.
